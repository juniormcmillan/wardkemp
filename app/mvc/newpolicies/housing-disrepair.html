What is a Plevin claim?

PPI Plevin claims are a reference to a case brought by Susan Plevin to recover the commission element of her original PPI claim. Susan Plevin won a landmark ruling by the Supreme Court in 2014. 72% of her Â£5,780 PPI premium, sold to her by Paragon Personal Finance, was a commission rather than a payment that would protect her. The Supreme Court ruled that this was also a form of mis-selling due to a failure to disclose the size of the commission. Mrs Plevin won her PPI case and the judgement has since affected thousands of people who have also been sold PPI policies in a similar way.

The rule to distinguish whether the claim is a Plevin claim, states that if over 50% of your PPI was a commission, you can make a claim.

Throughout the PPI scandal, the height of the commission levels found to be within a PPI payment reached 67%.
